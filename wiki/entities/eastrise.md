## Summary

EastRise Credit Union (formerly NEFCU) is the financial institution that holds the Marks Family Trust's mortgage, home equity line of credit (HELOC), and bank accounts. It appears in 48 emails via FTS and is a central entity in the trust's financial distress — the mortgage went into default in late 2024 and payment delinquency continued through 2025 under Jeanne Lavigne's trusteeship.

## Accounts Held

Based on email references:
1. **Mortgage** — on Trust property. Went 3+ months delinquent by January 2025.
2. **HELOC (Home Equity Line of Credit)** — separate from mortgage. Also had late payments. 1098 tax form issued for interest paid in 2024. Arik flagged potential error in the 1098 numbers.
3. **Bank accounts (non-loan)** — referenced as "non-loan accounts" with monthly statements.
4. **CPI insurance** — Collateral Protection Insurance placed by EastRise when homeowners insurance lapsed. Still unpaid as of December 2025.
