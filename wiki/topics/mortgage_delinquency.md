## Summary

The Marks Family Trust's mortgage and HELOC at EastRise Credit Union went delinquent beginning in late 2024, shortly after Robert Marks' death (October 28, 2024) and Jeanne Lavigne's assumption of active trust administration. The mortgage reached formal default status by December 21, 2024. As of December 2025, late payments continue and EastRise has placed Collateral Protection Insurance (CPI) on the property — which also remains unpaid.
