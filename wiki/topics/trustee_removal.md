## Summary

Arik Marks is pursuing the removal of Jeanne Lavigne as trustee of the Marks Family Revocable Trust, based on a documented pattern of breach of fiduciary duty — primarily non-payment of trust obligations (mortgage, HELOC, insurance, utilities) and non-communication with beneficiaries. The case escalated from informal complaints (late 2024) to attorney involvement (early 2025) to formal court proceedings (mid-2025), including an eviction case and motions for trustee/executor removal.
